AI TikTok Growth System

AI TikTok Growth System is a read-only analytics tool that helps creators review TikTok account performance and video-level trends.

How TikTok data is used

  • Login Kit is used to let users sign in securely with TikTok.
  • user.info.basic identifies the authenticated account.
  • user.info.profile and user.info.stats provide account context and top-level analytics.
  • video.list is used to retrieve the user's published video inventory.

The app is read-only and does not post, comment, message, or modify TikTok account content.
